About us

Our Philosophy

Jungle Cubs Gym is a unique non-competitive platform which promotes sports program designed to involve the children in sports. Jungle Cub Gym is a foundation of all sports, offering variety of programs intended to increase physical endurance and self-confidence in children 18months to 6years.

We aim at developing skills like confidence, flexibility, strength, Agility, Vigilance, balance, co-ordination, decision making.

Give your child the tools of lifetime of physical participation with Jungle cub pre-school sport program. We believe children build their physical fitness and confidence as they roll, turn, jump and tumble.
